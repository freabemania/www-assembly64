Assembly64... "All your artifacts are belongs to us"


A tool that helps you bulk download artifacts to your c64. Perfect for the use of 1541u2, sd2iec or just plain Vice on your pc


During first install of an artifact, thousands upon thousands of very small files will need

to be copied to your SD-card. This will potentially take many many hours so please be patient...

Next artifact update will however be brutally fast so only this first install will try your patience :-)

Assembly handles updates and only downloads the delta content between local and available content on the server. Smart huh?


Project can be followed at Assembly64 home on facebook
